In a blog post dated September 20th, 2023, Meta elaborated on the reasons behind the recent changes to the Facebook logo. The goal was simple: to forge a new design that radiates boldness, vitality, and resilience. Each modification was carefully selected to boost the overall design coherence and strengthen the app’s branding.
